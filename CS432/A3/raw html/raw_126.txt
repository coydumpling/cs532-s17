	<div class="wpb_text_column wpb_content_element ">
		<div class="wpb_wrapper">
			<p><img class="alignleft wp-image-1527" src="http://www.trunewsnet.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/images2-1.jpg" alt="" width="181" height="183" srcset="http://www.trunewsnet.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/images2-1.jpg 223w, http://www.trunewsnet.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/images2-1-148x150.jpg 148w" sizes="(max-width: 181px) 100vw, 181px" /> <strong><span style="font-family: book antiqua,palatino,serif; font-size: 14pt; color: #0000ff;">Is There A Shadow War Going On?</span></strong></p>
<p style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">There has been a lot of back and forth as to the Rash of Protests going on around the nation, including those at several Republican encumbrances &#8220;Town Halls&#8221; with their constitutes. Many have claimed that those in attendance are not from their districts and some are not even from their states. It has also been suggested that former President Obama and his associate and financier, billionaire George Soros, are behind this &#8220;Shadow War&#8221;. Here we will explore the facts (as we have witnessed them) and let you, our readers form your own conclusions.<br />
</span></p>

		</div>
	</div>
</div></div></div></div><div class="vc_row wpb_row vc_row-fluid"><div class="wpb_column vc_column_container vc_col-sm-4"><div class="vc_column-inner "><div class="wpb_wrapper">
	<div class="wpb_text_column wpb_content_element ">
		<div class="wpb_wrapper">
			<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="color: #000000;"><strong><span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;">Craigslist Ad&#8217;s, similar to those posted during the Presidential Campaign.</span></strong></span></p>
<p><img class="wp-image-1530 aligncenter" src="http://www.trunewsnet.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/C5Zc_oEUsAAoY4i.jpg-large.jpg" alt="" width="381" height="508" srcset="http://www.trunewsnet.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/C5Zc_oEUsAAoY4i.jpg-large.jpg 480w, http://www.trunewsnet.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/C5Zc_oEUsAAoY4i.jpg-large-113x150.jpg 113w, http://www.trunewsnet.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/C5Zc_oEUsAAoY4i.jpg-large-225x300.jpg 225w" sizes="(max-width: 381px) 100vw, 381px" /></p>

		</div>
	</div>
</div></div></div><div class="wpb_column vc_column_container vc_col-sm-8"><div class="vc_column-inner "><div class="wpb_wrapper">
	<div class="wpb_text_column wpb_content_element ">
		<div class="wpb_wrapper">
			<p style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">We have located Craigslist ads throughout the Country, similar to those posted during the Hillary Clinton campaign by Clinton campaign staffers, in States where Republican congressmen and woman were to hold &#8220;Town Hall&#8221; meetings. Several sources at these town halls also reported sighting buses delivering attendees to the Town Halls, while others followed in (presumably) their own vehicles. </span></p>
<p style="text-align: justify;"><img class="alignleft wp-image-1533" src="http://www.trunewsnet.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/ProtestersBussedIn2.jpg" alt="" width="149" height="70" srcset="http://www.trunewsnet.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/ProtestersBussedIn2.jpg 957w, http://www.trunewsnet.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/ProtestersBussedIn2-150x70.jpg 150w, http://www.trunewsnet.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/ProtestersBussedIn2-300x140.jpg 300w, http://www.trunewsnet.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/ProtestersBussedIn2-768x360.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 149px) 100vw, 149px" /></p>
<p style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">The Obama funded group &#8220;Organize for Action&#8221; has notable called for these town hall disruptions, even going so far as to publish instuction booklets on how to do so. Ironically, the techniques outlined in these &#8220;Instructional booklets&#8221; are the same ones being used at these town halls as has been reported by the nations mainstream media outlets. </span></p>

		</div>
	</div>

	<div class="wpb_text_column wpb_content_element ">
		<div class="wpb_wrapper">
			<p><img class="alignleft wp-image-1539" src="http://www.trunewsnet.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/OrginzedProtesters.jpg" alt="" width="351" height="225" srcset="http://www.trunewsnet.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/OrginzedProtesters.jpg 2048w, http://www.trunewsnet.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/OrginzedProtesters-150x96.jpg 150w, http://www.trunewsnet.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/OrginzedProtesters-300x192.jpg 300w, http://www.trunewsnet.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/OrginzedProtesters-768x492.jpg 768w, http://www.trunewsnet.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/02/OrginzedProtesters-1024x657.jpg 1024w" sizes="(max-width: 351px) 100vw, 351px" /> <span style="font-family: book antiqua,palatino,serif; font-size: 10pt; color: #0000ff;">The &#8220;Organize For Action&#8221; Facebook page dubbed the actions &#8220;Town Hall Project 2018&#8221;.</span></p>
<p style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">Another common theme seen during the presidential campaign, and in may of the nation wide  protests following the election, is the appearance of uniformly identical professionally printed protest signs. We [TRUNewsNet] do not believe that all of the town hall attendees are either paid or part of the organized group. However, according to the groups instructions&#8230;the organized attendees are strategically seated in the front rows and placed throughout the town hall audience to have the greatest impact and appearance.<br />
</span></p>

		</div>
	</div>
</div></div></div></div><div class="vc_row wpb_row vc_row-fluid"><div class="wpb_column vc_column_container vc_col-sm-12"><div class="vc_column-inner "><div class="wpb_wrapper">
	<div class="wpb_text_column wpb_content_element ">
		<div class="wpb_wrapper">
			<p style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">It is kind of hard to declare that those attending these town halls are not rehearsed when one person speaks and 50 more start chanting, in unison, the same catch phrase at the same time&#8230;as if on cue. It is clear that even, if not all, there is a good majority of organized activities to disrupt Republican activities that seem to be much like those which were employed by Obama associate Bob Cramer, the DNC and the Clinton campaign at Trump rallies prior to the election.<br />
</span></p>
<p style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;"><strong>FreeBeacon&#8217;s Joe Schoffstall</strong> reports that a group serving as a central hub of information for congressional town halls was founded by a former Hillary Clinton campaign staffer and its parent company is located at the same address as an<strong> organization funded by liberal billionaire George Soros</strong>.</span></p>
<p style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">The Town Hall Project, a group that serves as a one-stop shop of information for town hall protests across the country, is connected to a former Clinton staffer and is also closely affiliated with a number of liberal groups.</span></p>
<p style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;">The group, which has been referenced by media outlets on numerous occasions, bills itself as a &#8220;volunteer-powered, grassroots effort that empowers constituents across the country to have face-to-face conversations with their elected representatives.&#8221;</span></p>
<p style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;"><strong>Jimmy Dahman</strong>, a former field organizer for the Clinton campaign in Iowa, founded the Town Hall Project. Dahman claimed on CNN that the town hall events are &#8220;all organic and happening at the grassroots level.&#8221;</span></p>
<blockquote>
<div class="quote_end" style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: 12pt; color: #000000;"> The <strong>Action Network</strong> is located at the same Washington, D.C., address as United We Dream, the &#8220;largest immigrant youth-led organization in the nation.&#8221; United We Dream has received funding from<strong> liberal billionaire George Soros</strong>. It began organizing &#8220;sanctuary campus&#8221; anti-Trump protests shortly after the election.</span></div>
<p style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-family: arial,helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: 12pt;"><span style="color: #000000;">The Action Network also shares the same address as Change to Win, the labor organizing group</span>.</span></p>
</blockquote>
