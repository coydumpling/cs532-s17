Tampa Bay Lightning trades Syracuse Crunch forward to ArizonaThe Tampa Bay Lightning have acquired right wing Stefan Fournier from the Arizona Coyotes in exchange for Syracuse Crunch right wing Jeremy Morin.

Fournier, 6-foot-3, 226 pounds, has played in 29 games with the Tucson Roadrunners this season, recording two goals and four points.

He has played in 139 career AHL games with the Roadrunners, St. John's IceCaps, Springfield Falcons and Hamilton Bulldogs.

Morin, an Auburn native, had nine goals and 12 assists in 43 games for the Crunch this season.
