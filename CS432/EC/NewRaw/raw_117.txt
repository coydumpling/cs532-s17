					<div class="entry" id="ami-post-content">
						<p>The release of <strong>Hillary</strong> <strong>Clinton</strong>&#8216;s own tax records held few surprises for politicos, but RadarOnline.com has learned her extended family is hiding a shameful tax secret: <strong>Chelsea</strong>&#8216;s father-in-law, <strong>Ed</strong> <strong>Mezvinsky</strong>, has owed the IRS more than $200,000 for more than ten years!</p>
<p>According to tax records obtained by Radar, Mezvinsky was slapped with a federal tax lien totaling $201,934 on January 30, 2006.</p><div id="dfp-tag-sharethrough" class="dfp-slot dfp-container dfp-tag-sharethrough " data-position="sharethrough"></div><div id="dfp-tag-mobile_box" class="dfp-slot dfp-container dfp-tag-mobile_box mobile" data-position="mobile_box" style="width: 300px; margin:0 auto;"></div>
<p><a href="http://i1.wp.com/radaronline.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/hillary-clinton-tax-chelsea-father-in-law-ed-mezvinsky-embed.jpg/" rel="attachment wp-att-1296262"><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-1296262" src="http://i0.wp.com/radaronline.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/hillary-clinton-tax-chelsea-father-in-law-ed-mezvinsky-embed-300x129.jpg?resize=300%2C129" alt="Hillary Clinton Tax Chelsea Father In Law Ed Mezvinsky" srcset="http://i0.wp.com/radaronline.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/hillary-clinton-tax-chelsea-father-in-law-ed-mezvinsky-embed-300x129.jpg?resize=300%2C129 300w, http://radaronline.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/hillary-clinton-tax-chelsea-father-in-law-ed-mezvinsky-embed-150x65.jpg 150w, http://radaronline.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/hillary-clinton-tax-chelsea-father-in-law-ed-mezvinsky-embed-768x331.jpg 768w, http://radaronline.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/hillary-clinton-tax-chelsea-father-in-law-ed-mezvinsky-embed-600x259.jpg 600w, http://radaronline.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/hillary-clinton-tax-chelsea-father-in-law-ed-mezvinsky-embed-700x302.jpg 700w, http://radaronline.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/hillary-clinton-tax-chelsea-father-in-law-ed-mezvinsky-embed-551x238.jpg 551w, http://radaronline.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/hillary-clinton-tax-chelsea-father-in-law-ed-mezvinsky-embed-100x43.jpg 100w, http://radaronline.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/hillary-clinton-tax-chelsea-father-in-law-ed-mezvinsky-embed-50x22.jpg 50w, http://radaronline.com/wp-content/uploads/2016/05/hillary-clinton-tax-chelsea-father-in-law-ed-mezvinsky-embed.jpg 800w" sizes="(max-width: 300px) 100vw, 300px" data-recalc-dims="1" /></a></p>
<p>As of press time,records indicate he had not yet paid the bill.</p>
<p><a href="http://radaronline.com/exclusives/2014/11/chelsea-clinton-father-in-law-mark-mezvinsky-restitution/" target="_blank" msn="nolink">That&#8217;s hardly the first of Mezvinsky&#8217;s financial scandals,</a> however.</p>
<p>In 2002, Mezvinsky pleaded guilty to 31 counts of fraud after he orchestrated a $10 million Ponzi scheme to scam money from friends and family. He served five years in federal prison and was released in 2008.</p>
<p><a href="http://radaronline.com/photos/hillary-clinton-plastic-surgery-claims-photos/" target="_blank" msn="notext"><span style="font-weight: 400;">PHOTOS: Putting Her Best Face Forward! Hillary Clinton Had Plastic Surgery Makeover For White House Run, Top Surgeons Reveal</span></a></p>
<p>Meanwhile, <a href="http://radaronline.com/photos/chelsea-clinton-no-makeup-pregnant-baby-bump-pics/" target="_blank" msn="nolink">his son and Chelsea&#8217;s husband, </a><strong>Marc</strong>, is embroiled in a scandal of his own <a href="http://radaronline.com/exclusives/2016/05/hillary-clinton-son-in-law-shuts-down-failed-hedge-fund-clinton-foundation-lies/" target="_blank" msn="nolink">after the sudden shuttering of his hedge fund, Eaglevale.</a></p><div id="zergnet-widget-49654"></div>
<p>The fund invested in Greek bank stocks and bought government debt, hoping the country&#8217;s economy would bounce back and deliver profits. But that never happened, and almost 90% of the $25 million they raised from investors has been lost.</p><div class="spx-pub-wrapper spx-radaronline"><div class="spx-slot" id="swoopAd"></div></div>
<p>A rep for the Clintons did not respond to Radar&#8217;s request for comment.</p>					</div>
