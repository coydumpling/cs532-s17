<p>The liberal pundit Alan Colmes – known for his collegial approach to politics – has died after a brief illness, according to Fox News and several of Colmes’ news media colleagues.</p>
<p>Colmes<a href="https://twitter.com/FoxNews/status/834769367749509120" target="_blank"> was only</a> 66-years-old.</p>
<p>Colmes is best known for being the liberal half of the former warring pundit team, “Hannity &amp; Colmes.” On the air, in a segment announcing Colmes’ death, <a href="http://www.mediaite.com/online/breaking-alan-colmes-dead-at-66/" target="_blank">Sean Hannity described</a> Colmes as “one of the nicest, kindest, and most generous people.” It was one of many tributes that flowed in from Colmes’ news media colleagues, who widely regarded him as humble and kind in a world in which political debate has increasingly coarsened. The Associated Press dubbed Colmes the “amiable liberal foil” to Hannity’s conservatism.</p>
<p>Colmes leaves behind his professor wife of 13 years, <a href="http://heavy.com/news/2017/02/jocelyn-crowley-alan-colmes-wife-photo-spouse-monica-sister-elise-family-married-death-dead/">Jocelyn Elise Crowley</a>, who is the sister of GOP foreign policy adviser Monica Crowley.</p>
<p>Here’s what you need to know:</p>

This is why I’ve been out recently and will be out this week as well. But I will be back taking your calls as soon as I can,” <a href="http://radio.foxnews.com/2017/01/30/a-note-from-alan/" target="_blank">he wrote,</a> according to Fox radio.</p>
<p>Sean Hannity released this statement, given to Heavy by Fox News: “The Fox family, Dr. Jocelyn Colmes, Monica Crowley and me personally have lost one of life’s most decent, kind and wonderful people you’d ever want to meet. When Alan and I started Hannity and Colmes, there wasn’t a day that went by where we didn’t’ say we were two most fortunate men in all television. Despite major political differences, we forged a deep friendship. Alan, in the midst of great sickness and illness, showed the single greatest amount of courage I’ve ever seen. And through it all, he showed his incredible wit and humor that was Alan’s signature throughout his entire life. I’m truly heartbroken at the loss of a dear friend. Our thoughts and prayers are with Jocelyn, Monica and his entire family. He will be dearly missed.”</p>

<h2>2. The Cause of Death Was Lymphoma</h2>
<div class="embed-twitter">
<blockquote class="twitter-tweet" data-width="550">

<p>How did Colmes die? The cause of death was not immediately known as word started to break of Colmes’ death on the morning of February 23. However, later in the day, <a href="https://www.washingtonpost.com/national/alan-colmes-co-host-of-hannity-and-colmes-and-liberal-in-lions-den-of-fox-news-dies-at-66/2017/02/23/e95ddfae-f9d9-11e6-be05-1a3817ac21a5_story.html?utm_term=.038f91af94aa" target="_blank">The Washington Post reported</a> that Colmes died of lymphoma at a Manhattan hospital.</p>
<p><a href="http://people.com/tv/alan-colmes-sean-hannity-says-late-fox-news-alum-shielded-wife-jocelyn-crowley-illness/" target="_blank">According to People Magazine</a>, Colmes tried to shield his wife, Jocelyn, from his illness. “[When] we first talked about it, you know, typical Alan style, his first statement to me was, ‘I don’t want my wife to worry or be upset,’” Sean Hannity told the magazine. “He was thinking about her. <h2>5. Colmes Leaves Behind a Wife, Jocelyn Crowley, Who Also Works on Public Policy Issues</h2>
<div id="attachment_1400168" style="width: 252px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><img data-attachment-id="1400168" data-permalink="http://heavy.com/news/2017/02/jocelyn-crowley-alan-colmes-wife-photo-spouse-monica-sister-elise-family-married-death-dead/jocelyn/" data-orig-file="https://heavyeditorial.files.wordpress.com/2017/02/jocelyn.jpg?quality=65&amp;strip=all" data-orig-size="242,342" data-comments-opened="1" data-image-meta="{&quot;aperture&quot;:&quot;0&quot;,&quot;credit&quot;:&quot;&quot;,&quot;camera&quot;:&quot;&quot;,&quot;caption&quot;:&quot;&quot;,&quot;created_timestamp&quot;:&quot;0&quot;,&quot;copyright&quot;:&quot;&quot;,&quot;focal_length&quot;:&quot;0&quot;,&quot;iso&quot;:&quot;0&quot;,&quot;shutter_speed&quot;:&quot;0&quot;,&quot;title&quot;:&quot;&quot;,&quot;orientation&quot;:&quot;1&quot;}" data-image-title="jocelyn" data-image-description="" data-medium-file="https://heavyeditorial.files.wordpress.com/2017/02/jocelyn.jpg?quality=65&amp;strip=all&amp;w=212" data-large-file="https://heavyeditorial.files.wordpress.com/2017/02/jocelyn.jpg?quality=65&amp;strip=all&amp;w=242" src="https://heavyeditorial.files.wordpress.com/2017/02/jocelyn.jpg?quality=65&amp;strip=all&amp;strip=all" alt="jocelyn elise crowley, jocelyn crowley, alan colmes wife"   class="size-full wp-image-1400168"><p class="wp-caption-text">Jocelyn Elise Crowley. (Rutgers file photo)</p></div>
<p>Fox News reported that the the family confirmed Colmes had died and was asking for privacy. One thing Colmes shared with his wife, Jocelyn: A love of serious public policy issues. Jocelyn Elise Crowley is a professor at Rutgers University with a PhD from MIT.</p>
<p>Her Rutgers bio describes Jocelyn as a Professor at the Bloustein School, a member of the Graduate Faculty, Department of Political Science, Affiliated Faculty, Department of Women’s and Gender Studies, and Senior Faculty Fellow, John J. Heldrich Center for Workforce Development.</p>
<p>Jocelyn Crowley is an author, who has written books on fathers rights’ advocates and, more recently, <a href="http://www.dailymail.co.uk/news/article-4252958/Fox-News-contributor-Alan-Colmes-dies-aged-66.html#ixzz4ZWpgYoJI" target="_blank">is studying</a> “couples who divorce after the age of 50” for a book that will be called, “Gray Divorce: What We Lose and Gain from Mid-Life Splits.” She also wrote a book on mothers and workplace flexibility issues. The couple did not have children.</p>
<p>In 2011, the couple bought a Fifth Avenue apartment worth more than $1 million. Alan Colmes’ net worth is usually put at about $12 million.</p>
<p>Her sister, Monica Crowley, resigned from the Trump administration after plagiarism accusations surfaced.</p>
<p>In a statement, <a href="http://www.nydailynews.com/news/national/alan-colmes-tv-host-political-commentator-dead-66-article-1.2980171?utm_content=buffer407b4&amp;utm_medium=social&amp;utm_source=twitter.com&amp;utm_campaign=NYDailyNewsTw" target="_blank">Colmes’ family said</a>, “He was a great guy, brilliant, hysterical, and moral. He was fiercely loyal, and the only thing he loved more than his work was his life with Jocelyn.”</p>
<p>This post will be updated as more is known about Colmes’ death.</p>
